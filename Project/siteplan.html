Overview

The website plan serves as a blueprint for the final project. This document outlines the site name, purpose, target user scenarios, color schema, and typography choices. (Wireframes omitted by request.)

This plan is a living document and may evolve as the project is implemented.

1. Site Name

Garden Route Eats & Markets

  • Why this name? It captures a directory-style hub for local food, pop-up stalls, markets, and specials across the Garden Route region.
  • Optional domain availability: gardenroute-eats.co.za
  • Logo concept: A minimal wordmark with a produce icon.

2. Site Purpose

The site will help residents and visitors discover weekly market dates, vendor highlights, food specials, and directions. It will also include a simple contact form for vendors to apply for a stall and a newsletter signup for shoppers.
